KLICK

School trip to the former inner German border? Lame! Yassi, Lolo and Sina sneak off. Their goal: a nearby lake. In between: a former military area. Jokingly they start imagining what kinds of dangers could be waiting for them there. Suddenly one of them steps on something that makes a clicking sound. A landmine? No way.
