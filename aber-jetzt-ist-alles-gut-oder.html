Aber Jetzt ist Alles Gut, oder?

Other People Cope.
Two women. Copenhagen and Berlin. At dinner with the family and in a bar. A bicycle accident and a r*pe. 20 years ago and 2 years ago.
In this short documentary (20 mins, digital) two people share their experiences of traumatic events in their life. What are the differences? What is similar? Why is it so much harder to speak about rape? While one woman tells her story directly to her family at the dinner table, the other one shares her experiences with us through a computer voice narrating what is too hard to speak out loud.

“Do you think people are shocked when you tell them you had a bicycle accident?"
"I don’t think so. That’s not how I perceive it."
“It is also the word (r*pe), that is, I think, difficult"
